About us

Frans Scherpenisse founded Alconet in 1997, the creation took place in the Rotterdam Botlek. Alconet offers different services; a variety of services that are facilitated on the own depot, such as maintenance and renovation and the purchase and sales of a large number of container types every year. By using high quality materials and certified staff, we offer the highest possible service towards our customers.
In the past 15 years Alconet has grown into a business with a solid negotiating position in the market. In addition several modification programs are being used, we have fully equipped mobile workshops and we obtained many storage contracts successfully.
The primary advantage of Alconet is that we offer customer-oriented solutions; every customer has different wishes and needs and therefor requires a different approach. By keeping this in mind, we are able to offer every customer a suitable solution, provided that this is technically feasible, related to each container type. Alconet guarantees a high-quality product, in addition we act fast and we are flexible and reliable. You can trust us with your containers!
